# Story Forge Source Bank

This document is a planning bank for future Story Forge seeds.

It preserves source ideas before they are promoted into the app. Entries here are source-pattern notes, not finished seeds. Final stories should stay clearly fictional and inspired by source pressure, not treated as retellings.

Indigenous, religious, and living cultural traditions need extra care. Use them with context, avoid flattening living cultures into fantasy material, and keep the source boundary visible.

## Empire, War, and Battlefield Shock

Historical or mythic anchor: 1410 battle between the Polish-Lithuanian alliance and the Teutonic Knights  
Category: Empire, War, and Battlefield Shock  
Source type: medieval battle / military history
